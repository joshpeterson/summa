About the Summa Explorer

The Summa Explorer site is designed to make reading and understanding the Summa Theologica as easy as possible. You can explore the Summa in the follwing ways:

Browse

The highly structured layout of the Summa make for easy browsing of the text. The Summa is divided into parts, each part into treatises, each treatise into questions, and each question into articles. Each article focuses on a specific topic, and includes arguments for an against the position Saint Thomas takes. The Summa Explorer provides a clear context for each article, indicating its question, treatise, and part.
